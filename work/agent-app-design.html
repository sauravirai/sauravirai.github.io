App Store Gotchas (real ones)

  • Guideline 4.7 — agent apps need clear content controls; jailbreak/uncensored = rejection.
  • Sign in with Apple required if you offer Google/Facebook login.
  • Privacy Nutrition Label — be precise about what is sent to LLM providers.
  • Guideline 5.2.3 — no misleading "AI-generated" claims; disclose generated content.
  • iOS kills long requests — chunk over SSE, resume via background tasks + push.
  • First-token latency < 800ms or you bleed users. Groq/Cerebras when it matters.
  • IAP cut: 30% standard · 15% subs after year 1 · 15% under the $1M Small Business Program.

Pattern to notice: the breakout commercial winners (Cursor, Granola, Klarna's CS agent, Rufus) are vertical agents owning a specific workflow. Horizontal chat is a winner-take-most race already decided. New entrants are betting on depth, not breadth.
