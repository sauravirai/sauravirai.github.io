How I Work

Build first, then lead

Prototype before spec. Hands-on building earns credibility with the engineers who build it for real.

Platform over project

If we're solving it twice, we're building it wrong. Reusable capabilities compound; one-off projects don't.

Engineering empathy

Years as a developer and TPM mean I read system diagrams, debate API contracts, and call tech-debt bluffs.

Markets ≠ monoliths

Localization isn't translation — it's UX, regulation, payment rails, and culture. Build the platform; let markets compose.

Metrics, not vibes

Every initiative gets a hypothesis, a counter-metric, and a kill criterion. Celebrate outcomes, not features.

Grow people who outgrow you

The team outlives the org. I measure my success by the careers I've unblocked.
