About

The 30-second version of two decades.

I'm a product executive who has spent 22 years building and leading at the intersection of identity, customer engagement, monetization, and internationalization — at companies where every product decision ripples across hundreds of millions of customers.

Most recently at Walmart, I led a 55-person org across product, analytics, and program management, owning one of five global eCommerce domains for Walmart International (18 countries) and now driving the Global Identity, Profile, and Omnichannel Accounts platform for Walmart Inc.

Earlier I shipped Alexa Smart Home into India, owned Amazon Business's home page, modernized Nordstrom's customer identity onto AWS, and led ERP transformation across King County government. Engineering DNA at Oracle and Microsoft underpins how I think about platforms.

My leadership style is servant, candid, and outcome-obsessed. I'm at my best building from ambiguity, stitching cross-functional bets into platforms, and growing leaders who outgrow me.

How I lead

Patterns I keep coming back to.

🧭

Platform over project

If we're solving it twice, we're building it wrong. I bias hard toward reusable capabilities — identity, i18n, monetization — because compounding leverage is the executive's edge.

🤝

Servant leadership

The team I build outlives the org I sit in. I invest in mentorship, clear feedback, and structured talent reviews — and I measure my own success by the careers I've unblocked.

📐

Engineering empathy

Years as a developer and TPM mean I can read a system diagram, debate an API contract, and call a tech-debt bluff. Product strategy that ignores engineering reality doesn't ship.

🌍

Markets are not monoliths

18-market scale taught me that localization isn't translation — it's UX, regulation, payment rails, and culture. Build the platform; let markets compose.

📊

Metric, not vibes

Every initiative gets a measurable hypothesis, a counter-metric, and a kill criterion. We celebrate dollars saved and conversions lifted — not features shipped.

🪞

Self-aware by design

Hogan + Korn Ferry assessments inform how I show up — leaning into curiosity and decisiveness, watchful of perfectionism. I bring this self-awareness into how I coach others.
